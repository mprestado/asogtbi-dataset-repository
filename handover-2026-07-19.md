## Implementation Notes

- Google sign-in users are identified through `auth_provider = google`.
- Google sign-in syncs `name`, `email`, `google_sub`, `avatar_url`, and `email_verified_at` from the Google profile during login.
- Account settings now treats Google identity fields as read-only. Password controls remain available only for password sign-in accounts.
- Password accounts remain separate from Google accounts. An email already registered as a password account is not silently converted into a Google account.
- The request-access/register window is intentionally no longer a public self-service signup form; it explains how to request password credentials.
- Dashboard/navy standard is `--navy: #03558c`; avoid reintroducing `#17306f`, `#22439a`, or other purple-blue stops in dataset/dashboard surfaces.
